Working Experiences

HUAWEI Technologies Co., Ltd.

Intelligent Manufacturing and Precision Manufacturing R&D Engineer, June 2021 - Aug. 2021
Consumer Business Group, Desktop & Smart Office Accessories Development Dept.

  • I participated in the development of PixLab X1 (HUAWEI’s first printer product). My work was to develop an automatic detection tool (including techniques, e.g., image processing via OpenCV-Python, GUI development via Tkinter, Machine Learning Algorithm, etc.) for printing effects (e.g., line width, character darkness, roughness, blurriness, etc.) to make the detection on the production line more efficient and precise.

  • This was my first exposure to image signal processing. I learned relevant image processing algorithms (e.g., morphological transformation, contour detection, filtering, etc.) and applied them to the printing effects detection scenario.

  • Using my knowledge of machine learning, I modified some algorithms for better adaptability and performance, such as improving the efficiency of line contour detection by clustering algorithms.

  • The tool I developed was deployed on the production line and the Departments of Development & Test. As a result, our product’s printing effects far exceed those of HUAWEI's competitors at the final release. And I was appraised for The Best Intern Award at the end of this internship.
