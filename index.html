About Me

I am currently a senior student majoring in Statistics (with stream in Data Science) @ CUHK-Shenzhen. I am also a research assistant of CUHK-Shenzhen & SRIBD & HUAWEI joint laboratory—Innovation Laboratory of Future Network System Optimization, working with Prof. Zhiquan (Tom) Luo and Prof. Feng Yin on Wireless Network Traffic Prediction in High-dimensional Beam Space. I used to intern @ HUAWEI for two months as an Intelligent Manufacturing and Precision Manufacturing Research & Development Engineer. Recently, I am conducting a research with Prof. Yin's Ph.D. student Mr. Wenzhong Yan on Graph Neural Network (GNN) for Large-scale Network Localization.
